You went to the book store to buy fiction books. Each book costs $15 and you have a $20 discount coupon. The bookstore is also offering one free book with each purchase. Let's calculate the amount of money you have to pay.

Let B represent the number of books you bought.

The amount of money you owe = 15(B-1)-20

(1) If you bought 3 books, how much money would you owe?

(2) If you bought 4 books, how much money would you owe?

(3) If you bought 5 books, how much money would you owe?
