You are saving money to buy a bicycle. You have $20 to start with. Each week, you save $10 of your allowance. If you know the amount of time you have been saving, then you can figure out the amount of money you have saved.

(1) How much money will you have saved after 5 weeks?

(2) How much money will you have saved after 6 weeks?

(3) How much money will you have saved after 7 weeks?

(4) How many weeks will it take you to save $200?
